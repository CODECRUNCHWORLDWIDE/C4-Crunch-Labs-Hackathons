# Week 9 — Challenges

Optional stretch work. The challenges live *outside* the week's structured 11-hour target. None of them substitute for the mini-project; commit the `JUDGE-ROOM-LOG.md` and the mock-pitch take notes first, every time.

| Challenge | Skill rehearsed | Time | When |
|----------|------------------|------|------|
| [challenge-01-be-a-judge.md](./challenge-01-be-a-judge.md) | Apply the five-dimension rubric to another team's submission; produce a 12-item scorecard plus a one-positive-one-growth feedback note. Mirrors Lecture 3 §5 (the sidebar). | ~1h | Sunday evening or Monday morning of Week 10 |

## Voice

The challenges are graded against acceptance criteria, not against a numeric score. The goal is to produce an artifact you would be comfortable sending to a real cohort peer (or a real Devpost team) as feedback, not to score 12/12 on a checklist.
