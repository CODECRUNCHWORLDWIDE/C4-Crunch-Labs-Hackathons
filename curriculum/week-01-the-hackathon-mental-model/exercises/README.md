# Week 1 — Exercises

Short, focused drills. Each one should take 30–60 minutes. Do them in order — later ones reference earlier ones.

## Index

1. **[Exercise 1 — Personal brief](exercise-01-personal-brief.md)** — write the one-page brief that describes your strengths, default role, and stack preference. (~45 min)
2. **[Exercise 2 — Rate five real hackathon projects](exercise-02-rate-five-real-hackathon-projects.md)** — find five past Devpost winners and score them against the four-axis rubric. (~60 min)
3. **[Exercise 3 — Team contract draft](exercise-03-team-contract-draft.md)** — adapt the C4 team-contract template to the next event you plan to attend. (~45 min)

## How to work the exercises

- Read the prompt. Skim, don't memorize.
- **Write the answer yourself.** Do not paste from a template and edit; type it.
- Use the C4 voice — team-room, scope-discipline-first, blameless. No "rockstar" / "ninja" language.
- Commit each completed exercise to your Week 1 repo as you go. Small commits.
- If you get stuck for more than 10 minutes, skim Lecture 1 or 2 again.

These exercises do not have a "right answer" the way C1 exercises do. They are deliverables you will reference at every event. Take them seriously.
