# Week 1 — Challenges

The exercises drill the mental model. **The challenge stretches you into the rhythm of a real event.** It is optional, but if you can spare the 12 hours, it is the single most valuable thing you can do this week.

## Index

1. **[Challenge 1 — The 12-hour solo build](challenge-01-12-hour-solo-build.md)** — a deliberately small project, alone, on a 12-hour clock, with a MoSCoW chart and a recorded demo at the end. (~12 hours, split across a weekend)

The challenge is optional. If you skip it, you can still pass the week. If you do it, you will arrive at Week 3 (the team solo prototype sprint) with a feel for the clock that no amount of reading can give you.
