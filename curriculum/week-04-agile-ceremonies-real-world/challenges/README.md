# Week 4 — Challenges

The mini-project is the team contract template. **The challenge is the 30-minute scope-stress drill that tests whether the contract holds under pressure.** It is optional, but if you can find another C4 learner with a free 30-minute slot, it is the single best way to see your "no" script in motion before you face a real teammate at the real event.

## Index

1. **[Challenge 1 — Scope Stress Drill](challenge-01-scope-stress-drill.md)** — a 30-minute paired drill where one partner injects scope-creep requests on a clock and the other runs the "no" script. (~30 minutes of drill + 15 minutes of debrief)

The challenge is optional. If you skip it, the contract still passes Week 4. If you do the drill, you arrive at Week 5 with a calibrated sense of which of the three scope-creep shapes you handle worst — which is exactly what Week 5's scoping worksheet is going to ask you to pre-empt.
