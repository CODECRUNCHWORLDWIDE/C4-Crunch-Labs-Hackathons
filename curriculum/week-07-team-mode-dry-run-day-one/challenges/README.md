# Week 7 — Challenges (Optional)

One optional challenge this week. It lives outside the structured 12-hour weekly target. Take it on if the dry-run weekend went smoothly and you want to push the team-mode muscle further.

| # | Challenge | Time | Deliverable |
|---|-----------|------|-------------|
| 1 | [Clean 24 hours — acceptance criteria](./challenge-01-clean-24-hours.md) | ~1 hour | A self-graded acceptance scorecard appended to your `DAY-1-LOG.md` |

The challenge is *self-graded*. You apply a 20-item acceptance checklist to your own dry-run log and produce a yes/no score with one-sentence evidence per item. The output is the answer to "did the team run a clean 24 hours?"

Skipping the challenge does not affect the week's completion. Completing it produces a stronger artifact for the Week 8 retrospective.
