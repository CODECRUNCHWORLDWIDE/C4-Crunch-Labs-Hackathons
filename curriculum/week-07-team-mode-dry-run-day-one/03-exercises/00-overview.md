# Week 7 — Exercises

Three drills. Each is a *writing* exercise, not a coding exercise — the dry-run is the coding exercise (mini-project). The drills rehearse the lectures in writing so the dry-run weekend does not become the first time you write a team-charter, an hour-by-hour schedule, or a conflict intervention.

| # | Exercise | Time | Deliverable |
|---|----------|------|-------------|
| 1 | [Team charter for a fake event](./exercise-01-team-charter-for-fake-event.md) | ~1 hour | `EXERCISE-01-TEAM-CHARTER.md` |
| 2 | [Hour-by-hour schedule from a written prompt](./exercise-02-hour-by-hour-schedule.md) | ~45 minutes | `EXERCISE-02-SCHEDULE.md` |
| 3 | [Conflict-intervention drill](./exercise-03-conflict-intervention-drill.md) | ~45 minutes | `EXERCISE-03-INTERVENTIONS.md` |

Total: ~2.5 hours across Tuesday and Friday of the week.

The deliverables are committed to your week-7 working folder in the repo. Each exercise has an acceptance checklist; you grade your own work against the checklist before continuing.
