## Question 10

The named C4 *post-event close-out* is seven days long. Which of the following is the *Day 7* task?

- A. Send thank-you emails to all sponsors.
- B. Process vendor invoices and reconcile the financials.
- C. Publish the post-event blog, archive the Slack/Discord, and update the organisers' lessons-learned doc.
- D. Conduct the organisers' debrief.

---

## Answer key

1. **C — 7 days.** The carry-out window covers Day 0 (closing day photos) through Day 7 (thank-yous and close-out). Lecture 1 §1.

2. **B — Seven sections, starting with Problem statement.** Problem / Demo / Stack / Run locally / Screenshots / Team / License. Lecture 1 §2.

3. **B — Problem 15s / Demo 50s / Stack 15s / Ask 10s.** Lecture 1 §3. Adds to 90 seconds.

4. **D — at least $10,000 in confirmed sponsorship.** The four gates are venue, three co-organisers, eight weeks lead time, and *any* sponsor leads (not a specific dollar amount). Lecture 2 §1.

5. **B — Hardware lab, branded swag, insurance via MLH group policy, warm sponsor intros.** MLH does not pay events; it routes sponsors and provides operational support. Lecture 2 §3.

6. **B — three industry / three faculty-or-staff / three alumni-or-student.** Lecture 3 §2.

7. **B — Seven lines; line 7 is the close.** Intro / event / ask / audience / deliverables / tier-menu link / close. Lecture 3 §1.

8. **B — $15–$25 per attendee total food line for one-day events.** ($8–$12 per attendee *per meal*, two meals on a one-day event.) Lecture 2 §5.

9. **B — 1099 tax reporting plus team-equity disputes.** Plus the visible-value point — hardware sits on a desk for years; cash is invisible the day after. Lecture 3 §3.

10. **C — Publish the post-event blog, archive the Slack/Discord, update the organisers' lessons-learned doc.** Days 1, 4, and 0 cover sponsors, invoices, and debrief respectively. Lecture 3 §6.
